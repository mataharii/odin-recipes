Fried rice

Description

Fried rice is one the simplest meal you can make, basically just get whatever you have in the fridge and mix it with rice and soy sauce. Ta-da...

Ingredients

  • 3 cup of rice (preferably jasmine rice).
  • 1 medium shallot.
  • 1 clove of garlic.
  • 2 eggs.
  • 1 carrot, chop into half inch.
  • 2 sacllions, chopped.
  • 1 tbsp of oyster sauce.
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce.
  • 2tbsp vegetable oil.

Steps

  1. Cook the rice according to package, the best rice should be a day old rice.
  2. Heat a wok/pan and add the oil.
  3. Add the onion, garlic, carrot and scallion to the hot oil. Cook until soft.
  4. Set the onion on one side of the pan and add eggs, scramble.
  5. Once eggs are cooked; mix it to the onion. Add rice to pan.
  6. Rice should be mixed constantly to prevent burning on the bottom of the pan.
  7. Add oyster sauce and soy sauce to rice, continue mixing.
  8. After the sauce thoroughly mixed, the fried rice should be ready to serve. Enjoy!
