Shepperd Pie

Description

Do you want a comforting cold weather dinner? This is just might be the right answer for you!

Ingredients

  • 2lbs potato, cubed
  • 1-1.5lbs ground beef
  • 1 can whole corn, drained
  • 1 can cream style corn
  • 2tbsp butter
  • 1/4c milk
  • 1/5c onion, chopped
  • atsp of rosemary and thyme
  • 1 beef boullion
  • 1tbsp cornstarch
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Boil the potato in salt water.
  3. Brown the onion.
  4. Add ground beef until evenly browned, remove excess fat.
  5. Add beef boullion, salt and pepper and corn starch. Add the herbs.
  6. Once beef mixture is cooked, tranfer it to baking dish.
  7. Mix whole corn and cream style corn in a bowl and pour it on top of the beef layer.
  8. Mash the potato with butter and milk and layer it on top of the corn mixture.
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes.
