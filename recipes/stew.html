Beef Stew

Description

This beer and horseradish beef stew is the definition of pure comfort food!

Ingredients

  • 3lbs of beef stew meat
  • 5 Potato
  • 5 Carrot
  • 3 stalk of celery
  • 1 vidalia onion
  • 2 garlic
  • 1 tbsp of tomatoe puree
  • 2 bacon
  • beef stock, unsalted
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 2tbsp flour

Steps

  1. Cut the beef into 1/2 inch cubes.
  2. Mix the paprika, salt and pepper, and flour. Pour it on top of the beef and mix until coated evenly. Set aside.
  3. Cut the potato into 1/2 inch cubes.
  4. Cut the carrot into 1/2 inch cubes.
  5. Cut the celery into 1 inch long.
  6. Cut the onion into quarters.
  7. Smashed the garlic.
  8. Fry the bacon and torn into pieces, leave the bacon fat in the pan.
  9. Brown the beef with spices mixture, don't overcrowd. Transfer the browned beef into pressure cooker.
  10. Add the beef stock and salt and pepper to taste into pressure cooker and set on stew (approx 45 minutes).
  11. When timer is done, quick release the cooker and the stew should be ready to serve, make sure to check the salt.
